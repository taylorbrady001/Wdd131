About Valedracia

Valedracia is a realm of ancient magic, shifting alliances, and forgotten power. Set in a world where the veil between myth and reality is thin, kingdoms rise and fall by the will of dragons, spirits, and the brave souls who dare to shape fate. Whether you seek glory, knowledge, or revenge. Valedracia is yours to explore.
